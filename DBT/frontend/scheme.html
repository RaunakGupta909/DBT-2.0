Active Scholarship Schemes

Pradhan Mantri Uchchatar Shiksha Protsahan (PM-USP)

The Central Sector Scheme of Scholarship for College and University Students.

  • Scholarship up to **₹20,000 per annum** for PG/Professional courses.
  • For **meritorious students** from low-income families (below ₹4.5 lakh/year).
  • Merit list based on 10+2 Board results (top 80th percentile).

National Means Cum Merit Scholarship (NMMSS)

Aims to reduce the dropout rate of meritorious students at the secondary stage.

  • **₹12,000 per year** (₹1000/month) from Class 9 to Class 12.
  • For students from **Economically Weaker Sections (EWS)**.
  • Continuation subject to achieving 55% marks in the annual examination.
Apply on NSP Running

PM-YASASVI Scholarship Scheme

Scholarship for OBC, EBC, and DNT students studying in Class 9-12 and UG/PG.

  • High-value scholarship for **hostel & tuition fees reimbursement**.
  • Up to **₹75,000 to ₹1,25,000 per annum** depending on the course.
  • Mandatory entrance test (YASASVI Entrance Test).
Apply on NTA Portal Running

Post-Matric Scholarship for SC/ST/OBC

Financial assistance to students for pursuing education beyond Class 10.

  • Covers **mandatory non-refundable fees** and maintenance allowance.
  • **Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)** to the student's bank account.
  • Crucial for students pursuing ITI, Polytechnic, UG, and PG courses.

Upcoming & Focused Schemes

Vidya Lakshmi Education Loan Scheme

A unified portal for students seeking education loans and government subsidies.

  • Access to loans from various banks (**up to ₹10 Lakh** collateral-free).
  • Eligibility for **Central Sector Interest Subsidy Scheme (CSIS)**.
  • Single online application (CELAF) and live tracking of the loan status.
Visit Portal Ongoing (All Year)

PM Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) - Students

Skill Certification Scheme to equip youth with industry-relevant skills and vocational training.

  • **Free skill training** in high-demand sectors (e.g., IT, Automotive).
  • Training cost, assessment fee, and certification fee are covered.
  • **Placement assistance** provided post-certification.
Enroll for Training Enrolling Soon

Atal Innovation Mission (AIM) - Tinkering Labs

Promoting a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship in schools and colleges.

  • Access to **state-of-the-art technologies** (3D printing, Robotics) in schools (ATL).
  • **Mentorship** and exposure to product development and innovation challenges.
  • Student exchanges and opportunities to participate in national hackathons.
Explore Opportunities Ongoing (Yearly Challenges)

PM CARES for Children Scheme (COVID Affected)

Comprehensive support for children who lost parents/legal guardian due to COVID-19.

  • **Health insurance** cover of ₹5 Lakh under Ayushman Bharat.
  • Financial support of **₹10 Lakh** on attaining 23 years of age.
  • **Scholarship of ₹20,000** per child per annum for school-going children.

DBT Awareness Toolkit for Teachers

Welcome to the DBT Awareness Toolkit for Teachers. This page provides resources, teaching materials, and tools to help you educate students and parents about Direct Benefit Transfers (DBT) schemes. Use these materials to promote financial literacy and awareness in your classroom.

What is DBT?

Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) is a revolutionary initiative by the Government of India to transfer benefits directly to the beneficiaries' bank accounts. This ensures transparency, reduces leakages, and empowers citizens by providing financial inclusion and timely disbursements.

Benefits of DBT for Education

  • Financial Literacy: Helps students understand government schemes and financial management.
  • Timely Support: Ensures educational benefits reach students without delays.
  • Parental Involvement: Encourages parents to engage with school activities related to benefits.
  • Transparency: Teaches the importance of direct transfers in reducing corruption.
  • Empowerment: Prepares students for financial independence and informed citizenship.

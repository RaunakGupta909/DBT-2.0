Family DBT Benefits

Available DBT Benefits for Your Family

Scholarship for Students

Financial assistance for education expenses, including tuition fees, books, and uniforms for eligible students.

Eligibility: Students enrolled in government schools, Aadhaar linked.

Amount: Up to ₹5000 per year per child.

Nutritional Support

Subsidized meals and nutritional supplements to ensure healthy growth for children.

Eligibility: Families below poverty line, children under 14 years.

Amount: ₹1000 per month per child.

Health Insurance

Access to basic health insurance covering medical expenses for family members.

Eligibility: All registered families.

Coverage: Up to ₹50,000 per year.

Skill Development Programs

Training programs for parents and older children to enhance employability and income.

Eligibility: Families with income below threshold.

Benefits: Free training and certification.

What is DBT?

Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) is a government initiative to transfer subsidies and benefits directly to beneficiaries' bank accounts, reducing corruption and ensuring transparency.

How to Register

Register using your Aadhaar number and mobile OTP. Visit the portal and follow the simple steps to link your account and start receiving benefits.

Check Your Status

Log in to your dashboard to check DBT status, view transaction history, and ensure your benefits are being transferred correctly.

What is DBT?

Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) is a government initiative to transfer subsidies directly to bank accounts.

How to Register

Register using your Aadhaar number and mobile OTP. Visit the portal and follow simple steps.

Check Status

Log in to your dashboard to check DBT status and view transaction history.

Gramin Jagrukta Abhiyan

Special initiatives for rural citizens to ensure DBT benefits reach the last mile.

Bank Mitra / BC

In villages without bank branches, contact your 'Bank Mitra' (Business Correspondent). They carry a Micro-ATM device where you can link your Aadhaar using fingerprint.

Common Service Center

Visit your nearest CSC (Jan Seva Kendra). The Village Level Entrepreneur (VLE) can help you check your DBT status using the AEPS system.

Gram Sabha Awareness

Raise the issue of 'Pending Scholarships' in the next Gram Sabha meeting. The Panchayat Sachiv has the list of beneficiaries with failed payments.

No Internet? Check Offline.

Use the USSD service *99*99*1# from your Aadhaar-linked mobile number.

How it works:

  • Dial *99*99*1# on your dialer.
  • Enter your 12-digit Aadhaar Number.
  • Confirm the number.
  • Receive Bank Seeding Status via SMS.

Bank Seeding Letter Generator

Bankers often confuse 'Linking' with 'Seeding'. Generate a precise technical mandate form here.

To,

The Branch Manager,

[Bank Name],

[Branch Name] Branch

Subject: Application for Linking/Seeding Aadhaar in NPCI Mapper for DBT

Dear Sir/Madam,

I am maintaining a bank account number ................... with your branch. I voluntarily submit my Aadhaar number and give consent to map it at NPCI to enable me to receive Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) from the Government of India.

MANDATE CONFIRMATION

I specifically request you to SEED my Account No. ................... with NPCI Mapper. Please do not just do KYC linking.

My Details:

Name: ...................

Aadhaar No: ...................

Signature: __________________

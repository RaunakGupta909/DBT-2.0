Understanding Direct Benefit Transfer

What is Direct Benefit Transfer?

Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) is a revolutionary government initiative aimed at delivering social welfare benefits directly to beneficiaries' bank accounts. This system eliminates intermediaries and ensures that funds reach the intended recipients with maximum transparency and efficiency.

Our portal serves as a comprehensive platform connecting students, parents, teachers, volunteers, and government officials to streamline the DBT process and enhance accountability across all levels of administration.

Our Mission

We are committed to ensuring that every eligible citizen receives their entitled benefits without delay or corruption. By leveraging technology and digital infrastructure, we make the benefit distribution process seamless, transparent, and inclusive.

Through our platform, we facilitate awareness, registration, status tracking, and support for all stakeholders in the DBT ecosystem. Together, we build a more equitable and efficient welfare system for all.

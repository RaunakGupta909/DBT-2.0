Community Impact

Overview of DBT Community Impact

Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) has transformed the lives of millions in our community by ensuring transparent and direct delivery of government benefits. Here's how DBT is making a difference:

Key Impact Statistics

Beneficiaries Served

Over 45.6 million families have received benefits directly through DBT, eliminating intermediaries and reducing corruption.

Fraud Prevention

₹2,847 crore in fraudulent claims have been prevented, saving taxpayer money and ensuring benefits reach the intended recipients.

Transparency Improvement

100% transparency in benefit distribution, with real-time tracking and reporting available to all stakeholders.

Economic Empowerment

Empowered families to make better financial decisions, leading to improved education and health outcomes for children.
